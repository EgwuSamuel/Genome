What this score is — and is not

This is a research predisposition score, not a clinical or reproductive predictor. It cannot tell anyone whether they will have twins.

In the analysis behind this tool, these four European-discovered loci carried no twinning-specific signal beyond genome-wide ancestry (incremental R² ≈ 0). A percentile here reflects how many of these specific alleles a person carries relative to a reference panel — not a twinning probability. Do not use it for medical, fertility, or family-planning decisions.
